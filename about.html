About me

Good afternoon everyone. Today I would like to introduce myself. My name is Mykhailo Bulava, and I am a 19-year-old student. I am from Dnipro, Ukraine. I have been living in Tartu for the past four years. I graduated from Annelinna Gymnasium and I am currently enrolled at Tartu Vocational College, where I am studying software development. I chose this major because I have always been interested in how software and computer games work. I enjoy creating innovative projects and solving complex problems using programming languages to solve real-world problems. When I was twelve years old, I tried programming robots in C++ for the first time. During that experience, I learned how to work on projects both independently and as part of a team. Later, I attended Algopython courses, where I studied Python for four years. During that time, I also gained experience in web development. In addition, I created multiple 3D models in Blender. I continue to improve my skills in 3D design and regularly practice. Apart from programming, I enjoy creating physical models.Programming and gaming are also major interests of mine. I would describe myself as a friendly and calm person, but also ambitious and determined. I do not give up easily, even when things do not work the first time. My friends would describe me as helpful, hardworking, and reliable. In the future, I would like to establish my own IT company, where I could build international partnerships and create impactful digital products.
